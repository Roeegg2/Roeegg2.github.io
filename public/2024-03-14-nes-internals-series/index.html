Prologue

In this blog/series I will try to give an introduction to the NES architecture, directed to new emu devs looking to get started.

The NES has 4 major components: The CPU, the PPU, the APU, Cartridge & Mappers, and the Main Bus.

  • CPU - Chapter 1

  • PPU Background - Chapter 2

  • PPU Foreground - Chapter 3

  • Cartridge & Mappers - Chapter 4

  • Controls - Chapter 5

I do plan to write about the rest of the components as well in the future.

Disclaimer

This is not meant to be a substitute to documentation or anything like that. I try to provide simple explanation to each of the NES’s components, to make it easier to understand the NESdev wiki.

I’m not a professional by any means, and I still much more to learn. All the stuff written here is what I’ve learned and understood in my journey of writing an NES emulator. If you have found any mistakes, something isn’t clear, or just have some questions feel free to contact me at: Roeegg22@gmail.com. you can also checkout my Github page, the source code for my emulator is there
